Introduction to Algorithms

by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein

Genre: Academic / Computer Science

Published: 2009

Summary:

Summary:
This textbook, widely known as CLRS, offers a comprehensive introduction to the modern study of computer algorithms. It covers a broad range of algorithms in depth, yet makes their design and analysis accessible to all levels of readers.

Algorithms are at the heart of computing.
