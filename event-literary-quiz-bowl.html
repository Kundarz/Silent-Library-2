July 22, 2025 | 7:00 PM – 9:30 PM

Are you a literary genius in disguise? Put your book smarts to the ultimate test at the Literary Quiz Bowl, an electrifying competition where readers, thinkers, and trivia buffs face off in fast-paced, brainy rounds of literary questions. Whether you're an expert in classic novels, speculative fiction, banned books, or postmodern poetry, this event is your time to shine.

Hosted under the neon glow of Silent Library’s cyber-themed dome, the Literary Quiz Bowl merges intellectual curiosity with the thrill of competition. Participants will be grouped into teams and challenged across a variety of rounds — including rapid-fire, quote matchups, obscure character trivia, and “name that first line.” Each round is designed to test both memory and critical thinking.

This isn’t your average pub quiz. Expect glitch-themed buzzers, a scrolling LED scoreboard, and augmented literary flashcards that appear through the Silent Library app. Our AI-powered quizmaster will keep the questions flowing and the competition fierce. Bonus rounds include blackout poetry puzzles and redacted paragraph challenges.

No need to register with a team in advance — solo players will be matched on arrival. Costumes, literary puns, and team enthusiasm are highly encouraged. Winners will receive exclusive Silent Library merch, gift cards, and digital collector badges.

Come for the trivia, stay for the camaraderie. Spectators are welcome and can play along from the gallery using the Silent Library mobile interface. Light refreshments will be available, and the entire event will be livestreamed for remote attendees.
