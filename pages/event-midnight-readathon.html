July 29, 2025 | 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM

Ready to challenge your endurance and immerse yourself in stories that unfold under moonlight? Join us for the Midnight Readathon, a one-of-a-kind overnight reading experience where the pages never stop turning. From twilight to dawn, readers gather in the hushed glow of Silent Library for a nocturnal literary journey unlike any other.

Whether you bring your own book or choose from our curated “after-hours” shelf — featuring sci-fi thrillers, eerie mysteries, and dystopian classics — you’ll find the perfect companion for a night of quiet introspection and suspenseful storytelling. Attendees are encouraged to bring blankets, LED reading lights, and cozy attire. Silent Library transforms into a tranquil, neon-lit reading den, complete with beanbags, ambient soundscapes, and soft lighting zones.

Hourly “whisper prompts” will appear on the library screens — optional thought exercises or questions to ponder as you read. Those who complete a silent mini-challenge (e.g., finish a novella, reflect in a journal, or decode a hidden poem) will unlock limited-edition cyberpunk bookmarks and digital collectibles.

Midnight snacks and caffeine stations will be available throughout the night. At sunrise, gather with fellow readers to share insights and favorite passages during a quiet wrap-up circle. There will also be a sunrise meditation session led by our guest poet-in-residence.

This is more than just a readathon — it’s an act of quiet rebellion against the noise of everyday life. Come alone or bring a friend, but leave the outside world behind. The night belongs to the words.
